Friends of Coconut

We are a collective of farmers protecting and nurturing coconut agriculture. Coconut has been a part of our lives in many ways; us farmers and you consumers only together can protect its future.

What we stand for

Care

Run by people, we care about each person who helps us deliver our products to your table and try our best to provide them stability and security.

Commitment

The health and robustness of our biosphere is pivotal to our survival. We we work with farmers to reduce the impact on the environment as much as possible.

Quality

We strive to make sure that we deliver the taste and purity that you taste in your grandma's cooking.

Purity

Like a dew drop in the morning, we purchase highest quality untouched coconuts fresh from the farm. We process them without additives to get our products to your table.
